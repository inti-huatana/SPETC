\begin{abstract}
This guide explains how to install, configure and operate \spetc{}, the
spectro-photometric exposure time calculator, from the first launch to
photometric and spectroscopic planning, results interpretation and CSV
export. No knowledge of the internal physics is required; the companion
document \emph{Physics of the ETC} covers the models and formulae.
\end{abstract}
