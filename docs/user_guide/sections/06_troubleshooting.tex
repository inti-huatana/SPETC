\section{Troubleshooting}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\caption{Common messages and their resolution.}
\begin{tabular}{p{0.46\textwidth}p{0.48\textwidth}}
\toprule
Message / symptom & Cause and fix \\
\midrule
``\ldots covers X--Y \AA{}, but the requested calculation needs\ldots'' &
A curve (template, QE, filter, atmosphere) does not cover the requested
wavelengths. Narrow the range or load data with wider coverage; the ETC
never extrapolates silently. \\
\addlinespace
``Target altitude is below 5 deg'' &
The selected time puts the target too low. Move the time or check the
coordinates; the visibility plot shows when the target rises. \\
\addlinespace
``The template has no positive flux in the selected magnitude bandpass'' &
Reference filter outside the template's spectral range (e.g.\ $K$ band with
an optical-only spectrum). Choose a filter inside the template coverage. \\
\addlinespace
Required exposure flagged as saturating &
The target S/N needs a longer exposure than the brightest pixel allows.
Use the reported maximum unsaturated exposure and stack frames. \\
\addlinespace
S/N of a bright star lower than expected &
Scintillation ceiling (physical, not an error). Stack short exposures;
larger aperture and lower airmass raise the ceiling. \\
\addlinespace
``Resolve name'' fails &
SIMBAD needs internet; type coordinates manually --- nothing else requires
the network. \\
\addlinespace
Filters missing at start-up &
\code{data/filters.list} not found or empty. Each logical filter needs its
\code{\_Vega} and \code{\_AB} XML profiles; \code{BLANK.xml} in
\code{data/filters/} enables the unfiltered response. \\
\addlinespace
Slit width outside the calibrated range &
A measured slit-$R$ curve is loaded and the requested width lies outside
it. Change the width or remove the curve. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

If a run aborts with a Python traceback, the full text is shown in the
error dialog: it names the offending input explicitly and is the first
thing to attach to a bug report, together with
\code{etc\_user\_config.json}.
